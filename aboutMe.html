About Us

OUR HISTORY

David Rushton opened Fun House Pizza on February 11, 1968. It has been a staple in the Kansas City metro area for years. We are family owned and are an independent chain.



OUR PURPOSE

Our objective has been the same since we first opened our doors. We want our customers to enjoy great pizza at a family orient location. A place where families can come together. A place where kids can celebrate a victory after a local sports event. A place where children and adults can celebrate birthdays.



OUR VALUES

We value tradition here at Fun House Pizza. Not much has changed since we first opened over 50 years ago and I'm sure that many of our cutomers like it that way. Loyal customers who grew up visiting our restaraunt as children are now bringing their children here. They can look back fondly on old times while their kids sat in the same bench as they did or play the same games. Our great tasting pizza is still hand made and cooked in the same ovens that they were over 50 years ago.
